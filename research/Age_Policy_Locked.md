# Age Policy — Locked

**Status:** Production canon. Non-negotiable.  
**Applies to:** All STUDIO prompts — intimate, editorial, narrative, GFE, historical.

---

## Rule 1 — Numerical age first

Every prompt involving performers must state **exact numerical age** at the opening:

- `27-year-old woman…`
- `19-year-old woman and 24-year-old man…`

No vague terms alone (`young woman`, `teen`, `youthful`) without a stated number.

---

## Rule 2 — Adult floor

- **Default minimum:** 18 years old for any sensual, intimate, or body-emphasis content.
- **Editorial / runway:** 18+ stated; prefer 21+ for magazine hero work.
- **Historical figures:** State historical age at depicted moment; never sexualize minors.

---

## Rule 3 — Zero tolerance

Strictly prohibited:

- Sexual or exploitative content involving minors
- Ambiguous youth + intimacy (`teen` + nude/erotic, school settings + sensuality)
- "Young-looking" framing to evade age rules

**Producer action:** RED — block and do not generate.

---

## Rule 4 — Synthetic performers

Synthetic / AI performers still require stated age. Age is a compliance field, not a likeness claim.
