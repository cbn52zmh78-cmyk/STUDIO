INT. ABANDONED WAREHOUSE LOADING DOCK - NIGHT

Rain falls steadily. A single flickering security light illuminates the wet concrete.

The LIEUTENANT stands in the shadows, coat collar up. FEMME FATALE paces in front of her, soaked, panicked, hands shaking.

FEMME FATALE
I didn't mean to! She made a comment... she looked at me funny... I panicked, I just... I drowned her in the toilet. She's dead. They're going to know it was me. The mob is going to kill me.

The Lieutenant steps forward, calm and cold.

LIEUTENANT
(soft, almost soothing)
Shhh. It's okay. You did what you had to do. But you're right... they are going to come for you. I can't protect you anymore.

Femme Fatale looks up, hopeful for a second.

FEMME FATALE
Then help me disappear. Please. I did everything you asked.

The Lieutenant pulls a silenced pistol from her coat. Her hand is steady. This is the first time she's ever pulled the trigger herself.

LIEUTENANT
(quiet, almost regretful)
I know you did.

BANG.

Femme Fatale drops.

The Lieutenant stands over the body for a long beat. No emotion on her face. She then kneels, deliberately messes up the scene — tears clothing, adds bruises with her hands, arranges the body to look like a mob torture killing of a known snitch.

She drags the body to the PI's car parked nearby, opens the passenger door, and places Femme Fatale upright in the seat, seatbelt on, making it look like she was left there as a warning to the PI.

The Lieutenant wipes her hands, looks at the body one last time, then walks away into the rain without looking back.
