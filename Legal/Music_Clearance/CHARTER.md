# Music Clearance

## Two-sided licenses required
- **Sync license** — composition/publishing
- **Master use** — sound recording

## Workflow
1. Log temp track in `Music_Sound/Source_Music/`
2. Clear or replace before picture lock
3. Final cue sheet in `Music_Sound/Cue_Sheets/`

Uncleared music on client deliverable = Producer **RED** on release.
