# AI Content Compliance

## Covers
- Synthetic likeness of real persons
- Non-consensual intimate imagery (NCIM) — **always RED**
- Minors in sexual contexts — **always RED**
- Celebrity / public figure replication without license
- Platform AI disclosure requirements

## Gate 0 order
1. Run `legal_gate.py` **first** — before prompts, before generation, before client promises
2. Declare `--rating` and `--channels` (mass dissemination applies even to social-only)
3. Confirm talent `agency_ready` if cast appears on deliverable
4. Log verdict in `Gate_Reports/` and call sheet

## RED triggers (non-exhaustive)
- Deepfake of identifiable person without documented consent
- Sexual content involving minors (real or synthetic)
- Bypassing safety systems
