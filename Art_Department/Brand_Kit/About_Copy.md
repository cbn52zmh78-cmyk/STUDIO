## Long-Form Version — Website / Press Kit (~300 words)

**DAVID · The Archive** is a documentary YouTube channel produced by **Upon Tyne Productions** — a Newcastle-based AI-assisted production company specialising in premium historical and linguistic content.

At the centre of the channel is **DAVID** (Documentary Archive of Vocal Intelligence Data), a fully synthetic AI host. DAVID is not a persona layered over a human presenter — he is an entirely AI-generated character: voice, appearance, and performance produced by the STUDIO pipeline using Grok Imagine and associated render tools. Every episode is conceived, scripted, and editorially overseen by a human director; DAVID is the instrument, not the author.

**What DAVID does** is deceptively simple: he asks what the dead actually said, and then proves it.

Each episode is built corpus-first. The production team works from primary sources — inscriptions, papyri, manuscripts, grammarians, metrical poetry, and attested phonological records — before any line is written for DAVID to deliver. On-screen honesty labels mark every linguistic claim as ATTESTED, RECONSTRUCTED, or NOT ATTESTED. There is no hand-waving about "what the Romans probably sounded like." If the evidence supports the claim, it appears. If it doesn't, the uncertainty is disclosed on screen.

**Content pillars:**
- **Dead Languages** — Latin, Ancient Greek, Old English, Old Norse, Gothic, Sumerian, and the tongues time tried to silence. Launch slate: 6 episodes, Season 1.
- **Lost Civilizations** — Archaeology-led documentary exploring the cities, empires, and peoples who left records we are still learning to read.
- **Origin of Science** — The history of scientific ideas: how humanity learned to measure, predict, and explain the world, told through the people who first asked the right questions.

**AI disclosure:** DAVID is a synthetic AI host. No real persons are depicted or implied. All performances are AI-generated. Produced by Upon Tyne Productions under the STUDIO pipeline. Synthetic media is labelled per YouTube's AI-generated content disclosure policy.
