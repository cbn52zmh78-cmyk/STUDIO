## Reporting Lines

| Role | Reports to | Owns |
|------|------------|------|
| **Director / Screenwriter** | Producer (legal only) | Creative vision, script, performance direction |
| **Producer** | Studio | Legal gate, slate, clearance, schedule, kill decisions |
| **Legal Gate** | Producer | AI + IRL compliance review |
| **Talent Agency** | Producer | Performance studies, agency_ready |
| **Department Heads** | Producer via call sheets | Their module deliverables |
