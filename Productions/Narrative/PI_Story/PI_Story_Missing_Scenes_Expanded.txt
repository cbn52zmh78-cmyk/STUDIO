PI STORY - EXPANDED SCENES

SCENE A - FEMME FATALE KILLS THE OTHER GIRL

INT. CLUB BACKSTAGE BATHROOM - NIGHT

Femme Fatale is washing her hands, breathing hard. Another club girl (early 20s, chatty) comes in and starts fixing her makeup at the mirror.

CLUB GIRL
You okay? You look like you saw a ghost. That hitman thing got everyone freaked out...

Femme Fatale stares at her in the mirror. The comment hits wrong. Paranoia spikes.

FEMME FATALE
(quiet, dangerous)
What did you say?

Before the girl can answer, Femme Fatale grabs her by the hair and forces her head down into the toilet bowl. She drowns her. Sloppy, desperate, messy. The girl struggles. Femme Fatale keeps pushing until she stops moving.

Femme Fatale steps back, hands shaking, covered in water. She looks at the body, horrified. She quickly calls the Lieutenant.

FEMME FATALE
(into phone, panicking)
I fucked up... I killed one of the girls... she said something... I panicked...

SCENE B - THE LIEUTENANT KILLS FEMME FATALE + STAGES THE BODY

EXT. ABANDONED INDUSTRIAL AREA - NIGHT

Rain. The Lieutenant meets Femme Fatale under a flickering light near a loading dock. Femme Fatale is soaked, crying, in full panic.

FEMME FATALE
I did everything you asked... please help me disappear. I can't go back to the club.

The Lieutenant steps closer, voice soft.

LIEUTENANT
I know. It's okay. Come here.

She pulls Femme Fatale into a hug. Then, without hesitation, she draws a silenced pistol and shoots her in the chest.

Femme Fatale looks shocked. She drops.

The Lieutenant stands over her for a long beat. This is the first time she has ever pulled the trigger herself.

She then methodically stages the scene: tears clothing, adds bruises, arranges the body to look like a mob torture killing of a known snitch. She drags the body to the PI's parked car, opens the passenger door, seats Femme Fatale upright with the seatbelt on, and closes the door.

The Lieutenant wipes her hands, takes one last look, and walks away into the rain without looking back.
