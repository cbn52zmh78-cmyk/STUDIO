## THE 4 TONES

| Tone | Mark | Shape | Sound | Example |
|------|------|-------|-------|---------|
| 1st | ā | — flat | Hold a long steady "ahh" | mā (妈) mother |
| 2nd | á | / rising | "Huh?" going up | má (麻) hemp |
| 3rd | ǎ | ∨ dip-rise | "Hmm..." doubtfully | mǎ (马) horse |
| 4th | à | \ falling | A firm "No." | mà (骂) scold |
| Neutral | a | · light | Short and quick | ma (吗) question marker |

**Tone mark rule:** the mark sits on the vowel. If multiple vowels: a/e takes priority; otherwise the last vowel.

**Tone sandhi:** Two Tone 3 syllables in a row → first becomes Tone 2.  
Nǐ hǎo written as 3–3, spoken as Tone 2 + Tone 3. Don't change the writing.

## LESSON 2 — NUMBERS & TIME

**1–10:** yī · èr · sān · sì · wǔ · liù · qī · bā · jiǔ · shí  
**一 · 二 · 三 · 四 · 五 · 六 · 七 · 八 · 九 · 十**

**11–99 pattern:** [tens] + shí + [ones] → 35 = sān shí wǔ (三十五)  
**100:** yī bǎi (一百)  
**Two before measure words:** liǎng (两), not èr

| Pinyin | Char | English |
|--------|------|---------|
| xiànzài jǐ diǎn? | 现在几点？ | What time is it? |
| diǎn | 点 | o'clock |
| fēn | 分 | minute |
| bàn | 半 | half past |
| zǎoshang | 早上 | early morning |
| shàngwǔ | 上午 | AM |
| xiàwǔ | 下午 | PM |
| wǎnshàng | 晚上 | evening |

**Time pattern:** [AM/PM] + [hour] diǎn + [minutes] fēn  
3:30 PM → xiàwǔ sān diǎn bàn (下午三点半)

## LESSON 4 — QUESTION WORDS

| Pinyin | Char | English |
|--------|------|---------|
| shéi | 谁 | who |
| shénme | 什么 | what |
| nǎr | 哪儿 | where |
| wèishéme | 为什么 | why |
| zěnme | 怎么 | how |
| shénme shíhou | 什么时候 | when |
| jǐ | 几 | how many |
| zài | 在 | at / located |
| zhè / nà | 这 / 那 | this / that |
| gè | 个 | [measure word] |
| qù / lái | 去 / 来 | go / come |

**Word order rule:** NEVER flip word order for questions. The question word goes WHERE THE ANSWER WOULD GO.  
"Where is he?" → Tā zài nǎr? (他在哪儿？) — "He at where?"

## LESSON 5 — CONVERSATION & REPAIR

| Pinyin | Char | English |
|--------|------|---------|
| cóng | 从 | from |
| ne | 呢 | and you? |
| shuǐ | 水 | water |
| duì | 对 | correct |
| bú duì | 不对 | wrong |

**Repair strategies:**

| When you need... | Say... |
|-----------------|--------|
| Them to slow down | Nǐ néng màn yīdiǎn shuō ma? (你能慢一点说吗？) |
| To say you're lost | Wǒ tīng bù dǒng. (我听不懂。) |
| Them to repeat | Zài shuō yī biàn. (再说一遍。) |
| To say you get it | Wǒ míngbái le. (我明白了。) |

**Where are you from?**  
Wǒ cóng [place] lái. (我从[地方]来。) — I am from [place].
