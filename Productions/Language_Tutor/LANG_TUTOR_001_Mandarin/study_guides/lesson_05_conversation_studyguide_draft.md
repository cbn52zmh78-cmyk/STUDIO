## REPAIR STRATEGIES — KEEP THE CONVERSATION GOING

When you don't understand or need help, use these:

| Pinyin | Character | English | When to use it |
|--------|-----------|---------|----------------|
| Nǐ néng màn yīdiǎn shuō ma? | 你能慢一点说吗？ | Can you speak more slowly? | They're speaking too fast |
| Wǒ tīng bù dǒng. | 我听不懂。 | I don't understand. | You're lost |
| Zài shuō yī biàn. | 再说一遍。 | Say it again / one more time. | You missed it |
| Wǒ míngbái le. | 我明白了。 | I understand now. | You got it |
| Duì ma? | 对吗？ | Is that right? | Checking your own answer |

**A tip:** Native speakers appreciate that you're trying. Using "nǐ néng màn yīdiǎn shuō ma?" is a sign of a real learner, not a failure. Use it freely.

## HOW TO SAY WHERE YOU'RE FROM

**Pattern:** Wǒ cóng [place] lái. (我从[地方]来。) — *I come from [place].*

| Place | Mandarin | Pinyin |
|-------|----------|--------|
| America | 美国 | Měiguó |
| England / UK | 英国 | Yīngguó |
| Australia | 澳大利亚 | Àodàlìyà |
| Canada | 加拿大 | Jiānádà |
| [Your city] | (use as-is, or ask your AI tutor) | |

**Note:** You don't need to memorize country names now — just have your own answer ready. "Wǒ cóng [your country] lái." Learn to say your own country first.
