**FROM LESSON 5 — CONVERSATION AND REPAIR**

**The full beginner dialogue (annotated):**
A: Nǐ hǎo! (你好！) — Hello!
B: Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ shì shéi? (你好！你是谁？) — Hello! Who are you?
A: Wǒ shì [name]. Nǐ ne? (我是[名字]。你呢？) — I am [name]. And you?
B: Wǒ shì [name]. Nǐ cóng nǎr lái? (我是[名字]。你从哪儿来？) — I am [name]. Where are you from?
A: Wǒ cóng [place] lái. (我从[地方]来。) — I am from [place].
B: Hǎo de! Nǐ yào shénme? (好的！你要什么？) — Great! What do you want?
A: Wǒ yào mǎi shuǐ. Duōshǎo qián? (我要买水。多少钱？) — I want to buy water. How much?
B: Wǔ kuài. (五块。) — Five yuan.
A: Tài guì le. Piányi yīdiǎn? (太贵了。便宜一点？) — Too expensive. A little cheaper?
B: Hǎo de. Sān kuài. (好的。三块。) — Okay. Three yuan.
A: Xièxiè! Zàijiàn! (谢谢！再见！) — Thank you! Goodbye!

**REPAIR STRATEGIES (how to keep a conversation going when you get stuck)**
- Slow down: Nǐ néng màn yīdiǎn shuō ma? (你能慢一点说吗？)
- I don't get it: Wǒ tīng bù dǒng. (我听不懂。)
- Again please: Zài shuō yī biàn. (再说一遍。)
- Got it: Wǒ míngbái le. (我明白了。)
