- 11 = shí yī (十一) — "ten one"
- 20 = èr shí (二十) — "two ten"
- 35 = sān shí wǔ (三十五) — "three ten five"
- Pattern: [tens digit] + shí + [ones digit]

100 = yī bǎi (一百)

How to say times:

- 3:30 = sān diǎn bàn (三点半)

- 9 PM = wǎnshàng jiǔ diǎn (晚上九点)
