**Does this work with free AI tools?**  
Yes. The prompts work with the free tiers of Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot. No paid AI subscription required.

**Do I need to know anything about Mandarin before starting?**  
No. Lesson 1 starts from the very beginning — even the concept of tonal language is explained from scratch.

**Is the AI tutor actually useful?**  
Yes, meaningfully so. The prompts are written to give the AI your exact vocabulary and lesson history, so it can quiz you, correct your mistakes, and run conversations at the right level. It's not a gimmick — it extends the lesson into real practice.

**How long does it take?**  
Each video is ~3 minutes. With the study guide exercises, plan about 20–30 minutes per lesson. The full 5 lessons fit in a few hours spread over a week.

**What format are the files?**  
Videos: MP4. Study guides and bonus printables: PDF. AI prompts: plain text (.txt) — open in any text editor and copy-paste.
