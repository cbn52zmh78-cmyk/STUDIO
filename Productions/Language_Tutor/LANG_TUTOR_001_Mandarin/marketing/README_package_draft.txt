====================================================
WHAT'S IN THIS PACKAGE
====================================================

/videos/
  5 video lessons (~3 minutes each)
  Watch in order: Lesson 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5
  Format: MP4 (plays on any device)

/study_guides/
  5 companion PDFs — one per lesson
  Print them or keep open on a second screen while you watch
  Each guide has: vocabulary table, key phrases, practice exercises, and
  a "before next lesson" note

/ai_tutor_prompts/
  6 text files — one per lesson + one master prompt
  HOW TO USE:
    1. Open the .txt file for your current lesson
    2. Copy the SYSTEM PROMPT (everything between the lines)
    3. Paste it into Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot
       — in Claude: Projects > New Project > paste into Project Instructions
       — in ChatGPT: GPTs > Create > paste into Instructions
       — in Gemini or Copilot: paste as your first message
    4. Use one of the Conversation Starters at the bottom of the file
    5. Practice

  master_ongoing_tutor_prompt.txt = paste this once into a dedicated
  "Mandarin Practice" project after you finish all 5 lessons. Your
  permanent practice partner.

/bonus/
  tone_reference_card.pdf — all vocabulary + tone guide, one page, printable
  flashcard_sheet.pdf — cut apart and drill daily

====================================================
RECOMMENDED STUDY PLAN
====================================================

Day 1:  Watch Lesson 1. Read the study guide. Do the exercises.
        Paste the Lesson 1 AI prompt and run one conversation.

Day 2:  Review Lesson 1 flashcards. Watch Lesson 2. Study guide + exercises.
        Paste the Lesson 2 AI prompt. Practice numbers and time.

Day 3:  Quick review (Lessons 1–2). Watch Lesson 3. Study guide.
        Practice the market role-play with your AI tutor.

Day 4:  Watch Lesson 4. Study guide. Practice asking questions.
        Try combining Lessons 1–4 in one AI conversation.

Day 5:  Watch Lesson 5. Study guide. Run the full dialogue.
        Paste the master prompt and have your first open-ended
        conversation. See how far you've come since Day 1.

After Day 5: Use the master prompt daily or a few times a week.
             10 minutes of conversation practice beats one long session.
