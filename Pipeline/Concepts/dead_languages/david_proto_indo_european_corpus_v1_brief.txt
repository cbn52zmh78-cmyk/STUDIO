KEY CONCEPT: LARYNGEAL THEORY
Saussure (1879) proposed "sonantic coefficients" to explain vowel alternations in IE.
Hittite (deciphered 1915+) showed /ḫ/ where Saussure predicted laryngeals.
This is the most dramatic predictive confirmation in historical linguistics.
